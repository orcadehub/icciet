About Conference

ICCIET’24 has emerged as an enduring techno-platform to connect education experts and passionate educators all over the world for improving the potential for excellence in engineering education. It provides a premier interdisciplinary forum for researchers, engineers, academicians to present and discuss the most recent trends, innovations, concerns, practical challenges encountered, solutions adopted in the field of Computational Intelligence with its allied areas. The conference also aims to provide a platform for scientists, scholars, students from universities all around the world and the industry to present ongoing research activities and hence to foster research relations between the universities and the industry.
